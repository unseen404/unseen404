There are experiences people carry alone because there are few places to speak about them without shame, panic, or misunderstanding.

UNSEEN404 is a developing space for honest conversation about self-injury.

Not to moralize it.
Not to romanticize it.
But to approach it with clarity and harm awareness.

Many discussions about this subject collapse into extremes — either denial or sensationalism. Neither leaves much room for people to think clearly about what they are experiencing.

The intention here is simple:

reduce isolation
reduce unnecessary harm
create space for nuanced conversations that are often pushed out of public view.

The site is still under construction. More writing and material will appear here as the project develops.

If you found this page because you are trying to understand something that is difficult to talk about, you are not the only one asking these questions.
